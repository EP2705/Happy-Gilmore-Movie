Characters

 

 

Happy Gilmore
  • Played by Adam Sandler
  • Short-tempered and unsuccessful ice hockey player
  • Only skills are fighting and his powerful slapshot which he inherited from his late father
  • Turns to golf after being expelled from the hockey league.
  • Joins the professional golf tour to save his grandmother’s house from eviction
Shooter McGavin
  • Played by Christopher McDonald
  • Arrogant star golfer
  • Main antagonist of the film
  • Despises Happy Gilmore’s lack of golf etiquette
  • Feels threatened by Happy's rise to fame and tries to have him expelled or quit.
Virginia Venit
  • Played by Julie Bowen
  • Public relations head for the pro golf tour
  • Chosen to help Happy Gilmore keep his composure and behave more professionally.
  • Becomes Happy’s romantic interest
  • Understanding and supportive of Happy’s goal to save his grandmother’s house
Grandma
  • Played by Frances Bay
  • Raised Happy Gilmore after his father died
  • Has not paid her taxes for many years and owes $270,000 to the IRS
  • Her house which Happy’s grandfather “built with his bare hands”, is about to be seized
Chubbs
  • Played by Carl Weathers
  • Former professional golf tour star who lost a hand in an alligator attack
  • Meets Happy Gilmore at a driving range and is impressed by Happy’s powerful drive
  • Informs Happy about the significant prize money involved in the PGA Tour which motivates Happy to join
Otto
  • Played by Allen Covert
  • A homeless man who becomes Happy’s caddie on the tour
  • Becomes Happy’s best friend
Bob Barker
  • Played by Bob Barker
    • Best known as the long-running host of The Price is Right
  • Barker is Happy Gilmore’s golfing teammate in a Pepsi Pro-Am
  • When the pair come in last, Barker calls out Happy’s lack of skills
  • Leads to a hilarious fight in which Barker knocks out Happy
