Best Moments

Happy Gilmore is filled with many hilarious moments. The movie is known for its comedic scenes and memorable quotes. Below are some of the best moments of the film:

The Price is Wrong!

Midway during the golf tournament, a game is played in which participants are paired with celebrity partners. Happy is joined by Bob Barker, who is not happy with his reckless behaviour and excessive rage.

In the middle of the game, Happy fights Barker, after The Price Is Right host insults his golfing abilities. Barker even had the right one-liner - "The price is wrong"  to go with his beating.

Happy Rages after Missed Shots

Several times throughout the film, Happy throws his clubs after failing to make a putt or shot. These scenes are humorously over-the-top, with Happy's tantrums often including exaggerated gestures and outrageously furious emotions.

The ridiculousness of a professional golfer losing control to such an extent is a highlight of the film, with memorable scenes that leave viewers laughing and eagerly anticipating Happy's next tantrum.

Happy Fights The Alligator That Ate Chubbs’ Hand
Happy's tutor, Chubbs, is missing a hand after being bitten by an alligator. During one of his forays into the lake after hitting his ball in, he notices that the ball has been eaten by an alligator, the same alligator who took Chubbs' hand. So he wrestles with it and, after a violent underwater scuffle, wins the ball.

All in the Hips

Although Happy is great at whacking a golf ball like a hockey puck and launching it across the course, his weaknesses lie on the putting green, where he must show restraint and carefully knock the ball toward the hole.

Happy Fights his Caddy

When Happy first starts competing in the golf championship that will pay for his grandmother's house, he has no idea how the game is played.

When Happy's caddy picks up his clubs, he believes he is a sneaky flyboy trying to steal them, so he viciously attacks the poor child.
